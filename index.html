Bruno Mars

Music & Song Writer

Peter Gene Hernandez has born on October 8, 1985, known professionally as Bruno Mars, is an American singer-songwriter and record producer. He is known for his stage performances, retro shomanship, and for performing in a wide range of musical styles, including pop,R&B,funk,soul,reggae,disco, and rock. Mars is accomoanied by his band, The Hooligabs, who can play a variety of instruments, such as electric guitar, bass,piano,drums, and horns, and also serve as backup singer and dancers.
